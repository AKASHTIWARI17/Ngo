Who We Are

About Zenvyra Foundation

We are a passionate team of changemakers committed to creating lasting impact in underserved communities across India.

Empowering Communities Since 2018

Zenvyra Foundation was founded with a simple belief — every child deserves access to quality education, healthcare, and nutritious food regardless of their background. We work directly with grassroots communities to deliver sustainable programs.

Education Programs

Free schooling and skill development for 5,000+ children

Healthcare Access

Regular medical camps in remote villages across 8 states

Nutrition Support

Daily meals to 3,000+ malnourished children
